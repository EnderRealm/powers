---
description: List tickets with optional filters
---

Run `tk ls` with any filters provided by the user. Common filters:
- `--status=open` or `--status=closed` or `--status=in-progress`
- `--priority=0`

Priority status is:

0 - critical/highest priority
1 - high
2 - medium
3 - low
4 - backlog/lowest priorit
